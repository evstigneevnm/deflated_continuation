1 /*
2 General solver for the block linear system of size (n+1 \times n+1) (rank one update):
3 
4  GAX=GB
5 
6 where:
7 A=[LinearOperator d;c^T alpha];
8 X=[u v];
9 B=[b beta];
10 G=f(Preconditioner) is the preconditioner build by the Sherman Morrison formula f(.).
11 
12 input:
13 LinearOperator is a matrix-vector operator. In general it is a matrix of size nXn
14 d and c are contravariant vectors of size n;
15 b is an RHS contravariant vector of size n;
16 alpha and beta are scalars
17 Preconditioner is the preconditioner operator. In general it is a matrix such, that Preconditioner \seq inv(LinearOperator).
18 
19 output:
20 u is a contravariant vector of size n;
21 v is a scalar
22 
23 */
